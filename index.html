üìù How to Sync Data Between Devices

Step 1: On your first device, click "Export Data" to download your cat food data as a JSON file.
Step 2: Transfer the downloaded file to your second device (via email, cloud storage, etc.).
Step 3: On your second device, click "Import Data" and select the JSON file you exported.
Step 4: Your data will be merged - any new entries will be added while preserving existing ones.
üí° Pro Tips:
  • Export your data regularly as a backup
  • The import process will merge data, not overwrite it
  • Duplicate entries (same date, cat, brand, flavor) will be automatically detected and skipped
  • Always export before importing to have a backup of your current data
